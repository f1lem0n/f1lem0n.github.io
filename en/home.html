A glimpse at my offer

Data cleaning and preprocessing

I would argue that it is the most important aspect of any analysis. It also takes the most time and effort as it is a very tedious process and has to be adjusted to the data at hand. It has to be done properly and with attention to details to ensure that the results of the analysis are valid and reliable. I offer a full scope of data cleaning services, from simple data validation to complex data imputation.

Analysis and modelling

If given a dataset with rather a small number of features, one can perform a classic statistical analysis to gain insight into the phenomena which the data represents. On the other hand, if the dataset is large and complex, one can use machine learning or deep learning methods to extract information from it. I offer both types of analysis and modelling services based on the data you provide.

Visualization and reporting

The results of any analysis are only as good as the way they are presented. What would be the point of a great analysis if no one can understand it? I offer a full scope of visualization and reporting services, from simple plots and tables to interactive dashboards and reports in any of the following formats: PDF, HTML, LaTeX, Jupyter Notebooks and R Markdown. I can also help you prepare a presentation of your results for a conference or a meeting.
